## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Tagsi?
Tagsi is a mobile taxi-hailing app operating in Turkey. With one tap, you can call a taxi, track your driver on a live map, and pay securely.

### Is Tagsi safe?
Yes. All drivers undergo identity verification. Rides are tracked via GPS and 24/7 support is always available.

### Which cities does Tagsi serve?
Tagsi operates in 81 cities across Turkey, including Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir, Antalya, and major tourist destinations.

### What payment options are available?
You can pay with cash, credit card, or digital wallet (bank transfer). All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

### How fast does a Tagsi arrive?
Based on our user data, you can find a taxi within an average of 3 minutes.

### Is Tagsi available at Istanbul Airport?
Yes! Tagsi is available at Istanbul Airport (IST), Sabiha Gokcen Airport (SAW), and all major airports in Turkey.
